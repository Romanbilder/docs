# Welcome to the Future of Code Security

Clipron AI isn't just another static code analyzer. It's a living, learning platform that reveals vulnerabilities with **quantum-level depth**. We transcend traditional methods that rely on outdated signatures and surface-level scanning. Our proprietary technology, **Ron Cortex AI Engine 2.0**, analyzes your code as a complex, interconnected system, uncovering hidden threats and predicting attack vectors before they can be exploited.

<Note>
  **Production Ready**: Clipron AI is live at [clipron.com](https://clipron.com), protected by Cloudflare and optimized for performance.
</Note>

## What is Clipron AI?

Clipron AI is a production-ready, full-stack security platform that provides AI-powered code analysis. At its heart is the integration of several cutting-edge language models (Google Gemini, DeepSeek, Claude, OpenAI), intelligently routed by our Ron Cortex 2.0 engine.

## Analysis Levels

Choose the right level of analysis for your needs:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ron AI 2 Mini - Quick Scan" icon="zap">
    **Model**: Google Gemini Flash
    **Purpose**: Fast, cost-effective check for the most common vulnerabilities. Ideal for daily use and quick feedback in CI/CD pipelines.
    **Cost**: **Lowest credit consumption**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Ron AI 2 - Standard Audit" icon="search">
    **Model**: DeepSeek Coder V2 / Claude 3.5 Sonnet
    **Purpose**: Comprehensive and balanced analysis. Looks for complex logical errors and vulnerabilities. Suitable for regular security audits.
    **Cost**: **Medium credit consumption**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Ron AI 2 Ultra - Deep Audit" icon="microscope">
    **Model**: Claude 3.5 Sonnet / GPT-4 (fallback)
    **Purpose**: Deepest and most detailed analysis. Simulates complex attack vectors and analyzes code semantics. Intended for pre-production audits and critical components.
    **Cost**: **Highest credit consumption**
  </Accordion>
