## Step-by-Step Registration

### Option 1: Google OAuth

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Clipron AI">
    Go to [clipron.com](https://clipron.com) and click **"Sign Up"**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Google">
    Click the **"Continue with Google"** button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Grant permissions to Clipron AI in the Google authorization popup
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile">
    Your account is automatically created with your Google profile information
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Option 2: GitHub OAuth

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Clipron AI">
    Go to [clipron.com](https://clipron.com) and click **"Sign Up"**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose GitHub">
    Click the **"Continue with GitHub"** button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Grant permissions to Clipron AI in the GitHub authorization popup
  </Step>
  <Step title="Repository Access">
    Choose which repositories Clipron AI can access (you can modify this later)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Option 3: Email & Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Clipron AI">
    Go to [clipron.com](https://clipron.com) and click **"Sign Up"**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Registration Form">
    Enter your email address and create a strong password
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Check your email for a verification link and click it
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile">
    Add your name and other profile information
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account Security

### Password Requirements

If using email/password authentication:

- **Minimum length**: 8 characters
- **Required**: At least one uppercase letter
- **Required**: At least one lowercase letter  
- **Required**: At least one number
- **Recommended**: Special characters (!@#$%^&*)

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

<Warning>
  2FA is highly recommended for accounts with payment methods or sensitive repositories.
</Warning>

Enable 2FA in your account settings:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **"Enable Two-Factor Authentication"**
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
4. Enter the verification code
5. Save your backup codes in a secure location

### API Keys

For programmatic access to Clipron AI:

1. Navigate to **Settings** → **API Keys**
2. Click **"Generate New API Key"**
3. Copy and securely store your API key
4. Use the key in your API requests

<Note>
  API keys provide full access to your account. Never share them publicly or commit them to version control.
</Note>

## Account Limits

### Free Tier
- **Credits**: 50 per month
- **Analyses**: Unlimited (within credit limit)
- **Repository access**: Public repositories only
- **API rate limit**: 100 requests/hour

### Pro Tier ($19/month)
- **Credits**: 500 per month + additional purchases
- **Analyses**: Unlimited
- **Repository access**: Public and private repositories
- **API rate limit**: 1,000 requests/hour
- **Priority support**: Email support with 24h response

### Enterprise Tier (Custom pricing)
- **Credits**: Custom allocation
- **Analyses**: Unlimited
- **Repository access**: Full access + organization integration
- **API rate limit**: Custom limits
- **Dedicated support**: Phone and email support
- **Custom integrations**: Webhooks, SSO, custom deployments

## Managing Your Account

### Profile Settings

Update your profile information:

- **Name and email**: Basic profile information
- **Notification preferences**: Email alerts for analyses
- **Timezone**: For accurate timestamps in reports
- **Language**: Interface language (English, Czech, German)

### Billing Settings

Manage your subscription and payments:

- **Current plan**: View your active subscription
- **Payment methods**: Add/remove credit cards
- **Billing history**: Download invoices and receipts
- **Usage tracking**: Monitor credit consumption

### Privacy Settings

Control your data and privacy:

- **Data retention**: How long to keep analysis results
- **Repository permissions**: Which repos Clipron AI can access
- **Data sharing**: Opt-out of anonymized usage analytics
- **Account deletion**: Permanently delete your account and data

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Email verification not received">
    - Check your spam/junk folder
    - Ensure the email address is correct
    - Request a new verification email
    - Contact support if issues persist
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="OAuth authorization failed">
    - Clear your browser cache and cookies
    - Try a different browser or incognito mode
    - Check if you have ad blockers interfering
    - Ensure pop-ups are allowed for clipron.com
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Cannot access dashboard">
    - Verify your email address is confirmed
    - Try logging out and back in
    - Clear browser cache
    - Check if your account is suspended
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
